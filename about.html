Company Background

Alterflex Pty Ltd is an Australian-owned firm established in 2020, specialising in organisational change management, digital transformation advisory, and strategic stakeholder engagement for government and large enterprises.

Our consultants bring deep experience working across APS agencies, combining human-centred design principles, data-driven insights, and practical delivery experience to uplift change maturity, drive adoption, and deliver measurable business outcomes.

Mission Statement

“Our mission is to empower organisations to adapt, evolve, and thrive through strategic, people-centred change.”

Our Experience

Since its establishment, Alterflex has delivered services across multiple Australian Government agencies, including large-scale ICT transformation programs, regulatory reform initiatives, and high-visibility policy-driven change. We are known for our practical, people-focused approach and our ability to translate complex technical or policy requirements into meaningful organisational outcomes.
