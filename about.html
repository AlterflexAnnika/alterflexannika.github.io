Company background

Alterflex Pty Ltd is an Australian-owned firm established in 2020, specialising in organisational change management, digital transformation advisory, and strategic stakeholder engagement for government and large enterprises. We partner with clients to deliver complex ICT-enabled programs, combining evidence-based methodologies and human-centred design for sustainable adoption and measurable outcomes.

Our consultants bring deep experience working across a breadth of APS agencies ensuring a strong understanding of governance, risk, policy, and delivery constraints. We combine human-centred design principles, data-driven insights, and practical delivery experience to uplift change maturity, drive adoption, and deliver measurable business outcomes.

Mission statement

“Our mission is to empower organisations to adapt, evolve, and thrive through strategic, people-centred change.”

We deliver tailored, evidence-based solutions that reduce risk, maximise adoption, and ensure successful implementation of ICT and business transformation initiatives.

Our experience

Since its establishment, Alterflex has delivered services across multiple Australian Government agencies, including large-scale ICT transformation programs, regulatory reform initiatives, and high-visibility policy-driven change. We are known for our practical, people-focused approach and our ability to translate complex technical or policy requirements into meaningful organisational outcomes.

  • Human-centred change: Practical, people-focused approaches tailored to agency culture and constraints.
  • Scalable model: Direct engagement with the consultant delivering the outcomes, ensuring quality and accountability.
  • SFIA-aligned capability: Consistent competency levels, clear deliverables, and measurable outcomes.
